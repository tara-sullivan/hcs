Bibliography
Higher Education General Information Survey (HEGIS) VI:  Degrees and Other
Formal Awards Conferred Between July 1, 1970, and June 30, 1971

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 2138. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. 

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
icpsr-biblio-notify@umich.edu

Kerry; Rotondi, Matthew B.;  Murphy, Scott P., "Who are the early adopters
of new academic fields? Comparing four perspectives on the
institutionalization of degree granting programs in US four-year colleges
and Universities, 1970-2005." Higher Education. May 2011, 61, (5), 563 -
585. DOI: 10.1007/s10734-010-9349-z

2. Garcia, John A., "The race project: Researching race in the social
sciences researchers, measures, and scope of studies." Journal of Race,
Ethnicity, and Politics. Sep 2017, 2, (2), 300 - 346. DOI:
10.1017/rep.2017.15

3. Joslin, Randall S., "An Examination of the Diffusion of Associate Degree
Programs in Public Administration." Dissertation, University of La Verne,
2016.

4. Rojas, Rabio, "Social movement tactics, organizational change and the
spread of African-American studies." Social Forces. Jun 2006, 84, (4), 2147
- 2166. DOI: 10.1353/sof.2006.0107

5. Snyder, Thomas D., "Chartbook of Degrees Conferred, 1969-70 to 1993-94."
NCES 98-071, Washington, DC: United States Department of Education, Office
